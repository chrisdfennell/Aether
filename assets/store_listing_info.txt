Aether Luxury Analog


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 8000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Aether is a premium, luxury mechanical-style watch face designed for Garmin smartwatches. Inspired by the classic elegance of iconic Swiss luxury timepieces, it blends mechanical aesthetics with modern health metrics.

Key Features:
- Obsidian black radial metallic sunburst dial background.
- Gold-framed luminous baton hour markers and hand set.
- Custom 3-point gold crown at 12 o'clock (legally safe design).
- Magnifying "Cyclops" calendar date window at 3 o'clock.
- Symmetrical circular Steps Count sub-dial at 9 o'clock.
- Heart Rate (BPM) tracking centered on the lower dial.
- Procedural weather status icon (sun/cloud) showing current temperature at 6 o'clock.
- Elegant "Swiss Made" flanking details.
- Power-saving Always-On-Display (AOD) with burn-in protection.
